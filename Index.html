About Us

'Le-ger' The latin for 'To Read'.

We found our name in one of the founding languages of the world as we know it, Latin. As we aim to create an open and comfortable space for all readers, we decided to begin with a language that unites us all. Words of latin orignination can be found in many languages around the world, crossing borders a reaching many far and wide. As does literature. Legere could not be a more fitting name for the home of books.

We are a one-stop destination for all book lovers. Explore a wide range of books across genres and discover new ways to access your favourite reads. There is a book for everyone here at Legere and all are welcom to join us in the immersive world of literature.
